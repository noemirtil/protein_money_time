What is PMT?

PMT helps you find the best value nutrition at the supermarket. We analyze products to show you which ones give you the most protein and vitamins for your money.

How does it work?

Our smart scoring system considers:

  • Protein Score: High protein + Low fat + Low price = Better value
  • Vitamin Score: High vitamin C + Low sodium + Low price = Healthier choice

Where does the data come from?

All nutritional information comes from Open Food Facts, a free, open database of food products from around the world. Prices are collected and updated by our community of users.
